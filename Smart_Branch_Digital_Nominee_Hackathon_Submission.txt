Innovation Hackathon 1.0 – Idea Submission

BASIC DETAILS Team Name: SmartSecure Banking Solutions Idea Title: Smart
Branch & Digital Nominee Lifecycle System Theme: Financial Inclusion
using Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI)

1.  PROBLEM STATEMENT

Bank branches continue to experience heavy footfall due to routine
queries, nominee updates, and documentation issues that could be
resolved digitally. Customers often visit branches for simple
clarifications, resulting in long waiting times and operational strain
on staff.

Additionally, nominee management lacks proactive monitoring. Accounts
remain inactive for extended periods without nominee updates, creating
complications during claim settlements. In cases of nominee death, the
process is manual and time-consuming, leading to delays and reduced
transparency.

Existing systems do not provide structured branch load tracking,
intelligent digital query resolution, or a complete nominee lifecycle
management system integrated with verification workflows. This gap
impacts efficiency, trust, and financial inclusion—especially for senior
citizens and families.

2.  PROPOSED SOLUTION / IDEA DESCRIPTION

We propose a dual-module web-based solution integrated with existing
banking infrastructure.

Smart Branch Load & Intelligent Query Resolution System: Customers can
raise queries through a secure web portal before visiting a branch. The
system classifies queries as normal or branch-required. A dedicated
branch resolver can respond digitally in real time. If physical presence
is required, customers can book appointments based on live branch load
availability, reducing congestion and improving efficiency.

Digital Nominee Lifecycle & Will Management System: Customers can
digitally add nominees by entering their registered mobile number and
bank details (masked for security). Nominee addition requires
beneficiary confirmation. Both parent and nominee can view nominee
status in their dashboards.

If an account remains inactive for over one year, the Branch Manager can
trigger automated nominee update notifications. In case of nominee
death, the legal heir can upload the death certificate digitally. After
verification and compliance checks, funds can be released securely to
the verified beneficiary account.

3.  INNOVATION & UNIQUENESS

This solution combines branch load intelligence with a structured
digital nominee lifecycle system— an area currently managed manually. It
introduces proactive nominee update alerts for inactive accounts,
dual-side visibility for transparency, secure beneficiary confirmation,
and a digitally verified claim process.

By leveraging Digital Public Infrastructure principles such as secure
identity verification, document digitization, and interoperable systems,
the solution enhances accessibility, transparency, and financial
inclusion.

4.  TARGET USERS / BENEFICIARIES

Retail Banking Customers Senior Citizens and Families Nominees / Legal
Heirs Branch Managers Customer Service Executives Operations &
Compliance Teams

The system benefits both customers and internal stakeholders by reducing
manual workload, improving transparency, and strengthening trust in
banking processes.

5.  EXPECTED IMPACT / BENEFITS

Reduced branch footfall through digital query resolution Optimized
resource allocation using branch load analytics Faster nominee
verification and settlement Improved transparency in inheritance
processes Reduced legal disputes Enhanced financial inclusion Improved
customer satisfaction

6.  FEASIBILITY & IMPLEMENTATION APPROACH

The solution will be implemented as a secure web-based platform
integrated with the Core Banking System (CBS) through APIs.

Technology Stack: Frontend: Secure Web Application Backend: API-based
integration with CBS Database: Encrypted nominee lifecycle tracking
Authentication: OTP-based verification and role-based access control

The branch resolver dashboard will function as an internal portal.
Document upload and verification workflows will align with compliance
and regulatory standards, making the solution scalable and
cost-effective.

7.  ASSUMPTIONS & CONSTRAINTS

Customers must have registered mobile numbers linked to their bank
accounts. Regulatory approval and compliance verification are mandatory
for document processing. CBS integration may require phased deployment.
Strict data privacy and security standards must be maintained.

8.  FUTURE SCOPE / SCALABILITY

AI-based query classification Predictive branch analytics DigiLocker
integration for automated document validation Digital will registry
expansion Nationwide deployment across all branches to strengthen
financial inclusion using DPI.
